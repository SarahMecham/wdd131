Utah, United States of America

Utah was the 45th state admitted to the United States. It was named for the Ute tribe that lives in the region. In 1847 members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints began settling in the Salt Lake and surrounding area. Today Utah is best known for its abundant outdoor recreation. From the Wasatch Mountains in the north to the desert landscape and red rocks of the south. Visitors and residents can enjoy the breathtaking views and rich environment in a variety of ways.
